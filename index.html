We recognize the immense amount of information about climate change available and the sometimes confusing or misleading nature of it. We wanted to create an easy to use website that consolidated a lot of infomration, stories, and ways to help. This is a resource for anybody, just starting to get eduated or already deeply involved. It is never too late to learn more, it is just important to act now, because our world needs it. We hope this resource will help you learn about the multifaceted issue that is climate change and how to support the cause.
